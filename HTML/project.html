About us

Massive disorientation of cards and files have been observed in most hospital, where patients have to come very early in the morning to drop their cards, make a queue before their files are being sorted out and assembles to see a doctor not minding their seveirity of most cases.

Hence, the creation of this website to aid in curbing this problem by means of enabling patients have their database inputed on the website and they won't have to come early to summit cards and be on a queue for a long time just to get their files before seeing a doctor. They book online and when they intend to see a doctor, they book and get a time for an appointment.

This website assists in saving time, having easy access to doctor, and patients could even have a one on one online consultation if there is no need for physical examination with their doctor, incase they can't make it to the hospital easy booking and proper organization in hospital.
